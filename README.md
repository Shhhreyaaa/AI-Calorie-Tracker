# AI Calorie Tracker 🥗📸

A state-of-the-art AI-powered calorie tracking application designed to simplify nutritional monitoring. Using advanced Computer Vision and Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG), the tracker allows users to upload photos of their meals, receive instant ingredient and calorie breakdowns, and maintain an interactive logs dashboard.

## 🚀 Key Features

- **Visual Food Recognition**: Upload or take photos of meals for instant recognition.
- **Detailed Nutritional Breakdown**: Get protein, fat, carbs, and total calorie estimates.
- **RAG-Powered Diet Assistant**: Chat with an AI assistant that references your meal history to provide tailored nutritional guidance.
- **Premium User Dashboard**: Beautiful charts and analytics showing daily progress and historical intake.
